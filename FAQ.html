Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 11 AM to 10 PM from Monday to Saturday, and from 12 PM to 9 PM on Sunday.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan options on our menu. Please ask your server for recommendations.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a parking lot available for our customers.

Do you accept reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations. You can make a reservation by calling us or through our website.

Can I host a private event at your restaurant?

Yes, we have a private dining area available for events. Please contact us for more details.
